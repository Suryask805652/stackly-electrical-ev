Frequently Asked Questions

How long does charging take?

With our 350kW ultra-fast charging, you can charge from 10% to 80% in just 15-20 minutes. Home charging with a 22kW wallbox takes 4-6 hours for a full charge.

Can I schedule a test drive?

Absolutely! Fill out our contact form or call us directly to schedule a test drive at your convenience. We offer both virtual and in-person test drives.

What is the battery warranty?

All Stackly EV models come with an 8-year or 160,000 km battery warranty, whichever comes first, guaranteeing at least 70% battery capacity retention.

Do you have service centers?

Yes, we have authorized service centers in major cities across India. Our mobile service vans can also come to your location for routine maintenance.

How do I find charging stations?

Our mobile app shows all charging stations compatible with Stackly EVs, including real-time availability, pricing, and charging speed information.

Do you offer home charging installation?

Yes, we partner with certified electricians to install home charging stations. Our team can assess your home and provide a customized installation quote.
