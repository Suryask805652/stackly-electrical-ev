ABOUT STACKLY EV

Driving the future of mobility with innovation, sustainability, and passion.

Our Mission

At Stackly EV, we're on a mission to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable electric mobility. We believe that transportation should be clean, efficient, and exciting without compromising the planet.

Founded in 2020, we've grown from a small team of EV enthusiasts to a global community of innovators, engineers, and drivers who share a common vision: a future where every journey is powered by clean energy.

Our Story

What started as a small garage project in Salem has evolved into a movement. Our founder, a passionate engineer, envisioned electric vehicles that don't just replace petrol cars but redefine the driving experience entirely.

Today, we're proud to have a diverse team from around the world, all working towards a common goal: making electric mobility accessible, enjoyable, and sustainable for everyone.
